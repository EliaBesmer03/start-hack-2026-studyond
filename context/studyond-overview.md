# Studyond Overview

## What Studyond Is

Studyond is a three-sided marketplace connecting students, companies, and universities around real-world thesis topics, research projects, and talent sourcing.

- **Students** use Studyond for free to find thesis topics, interview partners, and career opportunities
- **Companies** pay a subscription to post topics, source talent, and access research capacity
- **Universities** partner for free -- Studyond integrates into their curricula and thesis workflows

## The Problem Studyond Solves

Most companies face two structural bottlenecks:

**Talent:** Access to universities is fragmented and expensive. Competition for top students is high, especially for SMEs. CVs and interviews are weak signals for actual job performance -- particularly at the early-career level.

**Innovation:** Innovation pressure is high, but structured innovation is unaffordable for most companies. Traditional formats (labs, chairs, consulting) work for the top 1% only. Finding the right research partner is hard, and there's no low-risk entry point into academic collaboration.

Studyond solves both by turning the thesis process into a structured talent pipeline and innovation channel.

## The Solution: "A Table with 3 Chairs"

Studyond connects those who care about the same topics and challenges:

- **As a student:** You work on a career-relevant thesis project and access valuable industry contacts
- **As an industry expert:** You unlock research capacity for the team and identify high-potential graduates (try and hire)
- **As a professor:** You build lasting industry relationships and develop graduates employers want

With one login, companies in Switzerland access students across 1,680+ study programs at 20+ universities -- 365 days a year.

## Structural Advantages

1. **Curriculum-embedded distribution.** Students use Studyond because their university integrates it into thesis modules -- not because of marketing. One program director embedding Studyond unlocks an entire student cohort.

2. **Three-sided network effects.** Students + companies + professors in one loop. No competitor connects all three.

3. **Thesis as hiring pipeline.** Companies don't just post topics -- they preview and recruit talent before candidates reach the open market. A thesis is a 6-month work-sample evaluation.

4. **Institutional lock-in.** HSG spin-off, ETH startup, co-creation with 10+ leading study programs. Pure-software competitors can copy features but not institutional relationships.

5. **Governance-first design.** Universities retain full academic control by default. No IT integration, no procurement, no curriculum changes required.

## Competitive Positioning

Studyond occupies a unique position: both highly embedded in universities AND high in ecosystem scope. 99.7% of European companies lack a central system for academic collaboration. No existing platform connects thesis students, companies, and professors across universities at scale.

Key competitors and how Studyond differs:

| Competitor | What they do | Studyond's advantage |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn Recruiter | Talent pool, brand awareness | Earlier access (thesis stage), work-sample evaluation, university-embedded |
| Career fairs | Face-to-face interaction | Continuous (not event-bound), deeper evaluation (6 months vs. 5 min) |
| Handshake | Career marketplace with university integration | Thesis collaboration + innovation capacity, not just job matching |
| Consulting firms | Deep expertise | Fraction of the cost, talent pipeline side effect, repeatable |
| The Forage | Virtual work experience | Real projects with real companies, not simulations |
